Introduction

Black Cat Analytics ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website tryblackcat.com and the dashboard application at airbnb-profit-dashboard.vercel.app (collectively, the "Service"). Black Cat Analytics is an Airbnb profit tracking tool designed to help short-term rental hosts understand their revenue, expenses, and overall profitability.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have over your data. We've written it in plain language because we believe you deserve to understand how your information is handled ‚Äî not just that it is handled.
